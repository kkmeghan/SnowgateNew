Intellisnow4.0

Focus on assets that need attention to maintain peak performance.

Overall Equipment Effectiveness(OEE)

OEE settings that maximise Quality,productivity and minimise cost and emissions.

Near-real-time monitoring

Near-real-time monitoring of equipment through sensor data using IIOT technique and intuitive dashboards enable operators to know the asset condition at any point of time.

Driven by AIML

AIML algorithms are being used to analyze sensor data from production equipment,identifying patterns that may indicate potential issues or impending failures. This allows for predicitive maintenance, minimizing unplanned downtime, and ensuring consistent production output.
